# **App Name**: VPK Nursing College

## Core Features:

- College Information Display: Display key information about Dr V P Kanakaraddi Memorial College Of Nursing including its affiliations, approvals, mission, vision and values.
- Faculty Directory: Showcase faculty members with profiles, including names, designations, bios, and specialization areas.
- Program Details: Outline details for the B.Sc Nursing program, including duration, eligibility criteria, admission process, and required documents.
- Gallery: Provide a photo gallery showcasing campus life, infrastructure, events, and activities.
- Admission Enquiry Form: Floating button to open a modal to collect student information and streamline the admission process.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Shades of calming blue (#3498DB) to convey trustworthiness and professionalism, fitting for a medical institution.
- Background color: Light grey (#F4F4F4) to maintain a clean, modern aesthetic without distracting from the content.
- Accent color: A slightly brighter, analogous blue (#2980B9) for interactive elements like buttons and links.
- Font: 'PT Sans', a modern humanist sans-serif for both headers and body to maintain clarity and readability throughout the website.
- Use flat, outlined icons in the primary blue color to represent college facilities, departments, and actions (e.g., phone, email, location).
- Use a clean and spacious layout with consistent spacing and alignment, combined with subtle card-based designs for sections to make information digestible.
- Incorporate subtle hover animations on cards and buttons for interactivity, enhancing the user experience without being overly distracting.
